Our EcoTech Solutions

Energy Storage and Battery Management Systems (BMS)

Advanced battery management systems (BMS) for lithium-ion and other energy storage solutions use IT to optimize battery health, charge cycles, and energy distribution.

IoT-Enabled Building Energy Management Systems (BEMS)

BEMS uses IoT sensors to monitor and control energy consumption in real-time within commercial and residential buildings. These systems can regulate lighting, HVAC, and other energy-consuming devices based on occupancy and need.

Smart Grid Technology

Smart grids use IoT, data analytics, and AI to make energy distribution more efficient. By monitoring energy flow in real-time, smart grids adjust power distribution based on demand, reducing energy losses.

Success Stories of IT

National Smart Grid Mission (NSGM)

Launched by the Government of India, the National Smart Grid Mission aims to modernize the Indian electricity grid using smart meters, advanced sensors, and data analytics.

Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC) Solar Power Project

The DMIC, one of India's largest infrastructure projects, incorporates solar energy and smart grid technologies to power industrial zones along the Delhi-Mumbai route.

India's Solar Parks Initiative

India has established large-scale solar parks in multiple states to encourage renewable energy production. Solar parks like Bhadla (Rajasthan) and Pavagada (Karnataka) are among the largest in the world.

Energy Efficiency Services Limited (EESL) and the UJALA Scheme

EESL, a government-owned company, launched the UJALA scheme to distribute energy-efficient LED bulbs and smart meters across India.

Solar Rooftop Program by Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE)

MNRE has promoted rooftop solar installations across the country, especially for commercial and residential buildings, supported by IT-enabled tracking and monitoring systems.

ISRO's Solar Calculator App

Developed by the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), the Solar Calculator app provides precise solar energy potential data for any location in India.

The Gujarat Solar Park (Charanka)

The Gujarat Solar Park in Charanka is one of India's largest solar parks, leveraging IT systems for energy management and monitoring.

KUSUM Scheme for Solar Agriculture Pumps

The Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (KUSUM) scheme aims to provide solar-powered pumps to farmers to reduce reliance on grid power for irrigation.

Andhra Pradesh Real-Time Energy Management (RTEM) Project

The Andhra Pradesh State Energy Conservation Mission (APSECM) launched the Real-Time Energy Management (RTEM) Project to monitor and optimize energy usage across government buildings and industrial units.

Insights and Innovations

In recent years, advancements in IT have driven transformative innovations in energy conservation and renewable energy. Smart grids, powered by IoT and AI, are modernizing electricity distribution, reducing transmission losses, and facilitating seamless integration of solar and wind power. Predictive analytics and digital twin technologies are enhancing energy efficiency by forecasting renewable energy production and optimizing energy use in buildings and industrial facilities. Blockchain-enabled microgrids allow peer-to-peer energy trading, fostering local renewable energy markets and reducing dependency on centralized grids
